CTTS 102: Basic Machine Learning

Subject Meaning and Importance

Meaning

Basic Machine Learning (ML) introduces students to the principles of Artificial Intelligence, focusing on how computers can learn patterns from data and make informed predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed. It covers foundational concepts such as supervised and unsupervised learning, classification, regression, and basic model evaluation.

Importance

  • Forms the foundation of modern intelligent technologies like recommendation engines, image recognition, and voice assistants.
  • Equips students with practical skills for real-world applications in IT, healthcare, business, and finance.
  • Enhances problem-solving and analytical skills through data-driven approaches.
  • Encourages innovation in areas like fraud detection, automation, and predictive systems.
  • Provides a stepping stone toward advanced AI fields such as deep learning and natural language processing.
Example: An e-commerce website applies machine learning to analyze user behavior and recommend products tailored to each customer’s preferences and purchase history.
